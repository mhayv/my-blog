Week Two Reflection

1. What went well in the design process?

I referred to last terms work and looked at the work templates we were given. I did a lot of that work to build and understand my persona and target audience, and I feel like that helped a lot when I went to work on my interviews. The work templates for analysing the interview answers [such as affinity maps, thematic analysis (which is currently still underway..)], also helped when I had to find insights from the results. It felt satisfying to see how habits, processes, and templates from the previous term have helped me in developing this terms work - I feel they made this week’s work more manageable.

2. Did you accomplish your sprint goal? Why/Why not?

My sprint goal was to complete and analyse interviews with people in my target audience, so I have accomplished this weeks sprint goal. I’ve done this through having questions set up and interviews conducted early in the week, leaving lots of time for analysis later on.

3. What didnt go well in the design process?

I think I may have overestimated how long certain processes would take me [for example, thematic analysis is taking more time than I expected], so I’d say being overly ambitious with what I could achieve within each week, and each day was something that didn’t go so well. I felt a bit rushed with the timeline I set for myself, since the week was going by quite quickly, but I was still working on the same few tasks.

4. What can be improved? What lessons have you learnt?

I’ve learned that I should set out tasks with more spacing, in case I do take more time than anticipated to complete it. This way, I won’t put unnecessary time pressure on myself.

5. What actions will I take for the next sprint?

For the next sprint, I will readjust my term plan, so I can stay flexible if tasks end up taking a longer time than expected. I’ll also try to organise workload with tasks that take a long time to be completed in the earlier part of the week, so I don’t rush it to the end of the week.
